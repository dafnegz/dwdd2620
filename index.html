Pothos Plant

Description:

Pothos plants have glossy heart shaped leaves that can be veriegated. It's vines are ideal for hanging baskets due to their trailing nature. Pothos plants are a beginner friendly plant, they prefer bright indirect light but can also be placed in low-light areas, water when the soil is dry.
